Blue-and-yellow macaw

Macaws are a huge group of neotropical parrots, they inhabit mostly forests and woodlands of tropical South America.

  • Scientific Name: Ara ararauna
  • Average Length: 81 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 32 years
  • Habitat: Tropical forests

Blue-green wings and tail, with a green forehead and yellow under-parts; face is white with a black beak and dark-blue chin. There are little variations in plumage with a more orange color in the underside.
